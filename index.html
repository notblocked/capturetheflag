WEB (Where Everybody Belongs)

WEB is a transition program that welcomes 6th graders.  The program is designed to help them feel comfortable as they begin  their middle school experience.

Click on WEB for more info.

Applications for the 2022-2023 school year are now open. 7th graders please fill out this form by Friday, May 13th.

Searching for Watch DOGS Volunteer

Calling all Northshore Middle School Dads, Grandfathers, Uncles, and father figures!

WATCH D.O.G.S. is an innovative father involvement, educational initiative of the National Center for Fathering. The goal is to provide positive male role models for the students, demonstrating by their presence that education is important.
