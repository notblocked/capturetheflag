Google + Hāpara = Better together

Mission-critical classroom management software for Chromebooks. Powering learning anywhere, anytime

Are you ready for seamless learning face to face or remote? Do your tech tools impact learning? Do your teachers have the tools they need to evolve their digital practice? Is your school district teaching students in the way they want to learn?

We have the classroom management software teachers and educational leaders need to transform learning. Hāpara makes digital instruction and learning flexible, engaging and safe.

Your K-12 homebase with rich lessons and activities, deeper learning experiences and a place that makes sense of all those apps

Hāpara offers a rich classroom learning ecosystem built into our Chromebook management for schools. Access thousands of customizable curriculum-aligned lessons and resources. Smoothly deliver differentiated and personalized learning in the classroom or at home to help meet the needs of all students.

Focus students while building digital citizenship skills

Hāpara’s Chromebook classroom management tools give teachers visibility into their students’ browser activity to help them become independent digital learners. That means teachers are able to use instructional time effectively while students learn to navigate online environments responsibly. With positive guidance, teachers can empower every student to stay safe and productive during digital learning.

Make Google Workspace a place for real learning experiences

Hāpara gives teachers seamless access to learners’ work in Google Workspace for timely and consistent formative feedback. With one click, teachers can see exactly how students are learning and give them the support they need to grow their skills and knowledge.

Streamline and simplify student workflows

Bring all your Google Workspace tools, edtech apps and resources together into one awesome classroom workflow with Hāpara’s classroom management software. Help learners get organized and strengthen their executive functioning skills with a single mobile-friendly tool. A streamlined workflow helps students strengthen the skills they need to become lifelong learners without being distracted by digital noise.

Keep learners safe

With tools that are easy to install and monitor, Hāpara helps school districts and teachers keep students safe any time they are learning online. Whether in school or remote, students are safeguarded when researching online, working on digital assignments or learning during a synchronous class session. With real-time AI technology for school districts and dynamic tools for teachers, the school community can ensure learners are protected.

Best edtech value

Hāpara’s classroom management software offers the best value in K-12 edtech. Instead of needing to adopt several standalone digital tools, with Hāpara, schools and districts have everything they need to provide a transformative digital learning experience.

Your partner in learning

We’re committed to your success with one-on-one onboarding, support, professional learning and certification programs, and a community built to support teachers, tech admins and decision makers throughout the journey of meeting learner needs.
